About
BikeWay

Welcome to BikeWay, your go-to resource for finding the best bike routes and bike-sharing locations on the map. Our team is passionate about promoting the use of bicycles as a sustainable and healthy mode of transport.

Our
Beliefs

At BikeWay, we believe bicycles are a vital solution to the pressing challenges our cities and communities face today. They offer a

clean

and

efficient

mode of transportation that helps alleviate traffic congestion and reduce air pollution, all while promoting physical activity and enhancing overall well-being. Our platform simplifies the process of locating and utilizing bicycles, making it easier than ever to choose cycling as your preferred way to travel.

Our mission is to empower the bicycle movement worldwide by ensuring access and practicality for everyone. We are dedicated to delivering an exceptional user experience and continually updating our features to help you effortlessly discover the best bike routes and bike-sharing stations.

Climate Change?

Bicycles not only offer a clean and efficient way to get around, but they also play an important role in reducing carbon emissions. By choosing to bike instead of driving, we can significantly reduce the amount of CO2 released into the atmosphere.
